Hamdi Dërvodeli

Hi there! I am a second year PhD student with an interest in the interplay between Tropical and Algebraic Geometry. My advisor is Diane Maclagan.

I completed my MSc in Mathematics at Kyoto University in Japan under the supervision of Kazuhiko Yamaki. My master's thesis was about construction of tropical morphisms from tropical modifications of non-hyperelliptic genus 3 metric graphs with tree gonality 3 to metric trees. You can find it here.

I earned my BSc in Mathematics at University of Prishtina in Kosovo.
